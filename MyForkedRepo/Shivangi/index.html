Pav Bhaji
A popular street food from Maharashtra consisting of a spicy vegetable mash served with soft bread rolls.
Pasta
Pasta is a type of food, traditionally made from an unleavened dough of wheat flour, water, and sometimes eggs, formed into various shapes and cooked by boiling or baking.
Pani Puri
Golgappa, also known as pani puri, is a popular Indian street food consisting of a hollow, crispy-fried ball filled with a mixture of potatoes, chickpeas, onions, spices, and then dipped in flavored water, usually tamarind or mint.
Masala Dosa
A South Indian specialty consisting of a thin crispy crepe filled with spiced potato filling.
Samosa
A samosa is a popular fried or baked pastry snack, originating from the Middle East and now a staple in South Asian and West Asian cuisine, typically filled with spiced potatoes, peas, and other vegetables.
Chole Bhature
Chole Bhature is a popular North Indian dish consisting of spicy chickpeas (chole) served with deep-fried bread (bhatura), often enjoyed as a breakfast or lunch meal, and can be accompanied by pickled onions and green chutney.
