Veg White Pasta

A creamy and delicious pasta dish with vegetables in a white sauce.

Ingredients

  • 250g pasta (penne, fusilli, or your choice)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 4-5 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 1 medium carrot, diced
  • 1 medium bell pepper, diced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Directions

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pan, heat olive oil and butter over medium heat. Add minced garlic and sauté for 30 seconds until fragrant.
  3. Add chopped onions and sauté until translucent, about 2-3 minutes.
  4. Add diced carrots and cook for 2 minutes, then add bell peppers, broccoli, and mushrooms. Cook for another 3-4 minutes until vegetables are slightly tender but still crisp.
  5. Push the vegetables to one side of the pan and add flour to the empty space. Stir the flour for 1-2 minutes to cook it.
  6. Gradually pour in milk while continuously stirring to avoid lumps. Mix the vegetables with the sauce.
  7. Add heavy cream, dried oregano, dried basil, black pepper, and salt. Stir well and let the sauce simmer for 2-3 minutes until it thickens.
  8. Add grated parmesan cheese and stir until melted and incorporated into the sauce.
  9. Add the cooked pasta to the sauce and toss well to coat all the pasta with the sauce.
  10. Cook for another 1-2 minutes to let the pasta absorb some of the flavors.
  11. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve hot.
